There are four types of financial statements. The first one is the balances sheet that equates assets and liabilities. The second one is the income statement that takes into account the revenue and the expenses and matches them. The third one is the cash flow statement that looks at the cash transactions and non-cash transactions and finds out about the cash inflow and cash outflow.
